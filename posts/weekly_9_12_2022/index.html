weekly analysis 9/12/2022

Sep 12, 2022
#crypto #weekly #Bitcoin
  • BTC price dipped close to 18,500 USD this week, as expected in the post for September 5, 2022.
  • Price is still following ealy 2019 price action.
  • Weekly Heikin Ashi candle at current price is green.
  • If candle remains green by September 19, 2022, then price could trend higher over the next weeks mirroring early 2019 price action.
  • DXY (dollar index) appears to have topped.
  • Bearish divergence on weekly RSI and MACD.
  • A down trend in DXY is usually posative for BTC price.
  • BTC dominance might have bottomed and could incease from here.
  • Many, including me, associate an increase in BTC dominance with a decrease in the overall crypto market. However, Dave the wave likens the BTC dominance to the gold-to-silver ratio, and cautions not to correlate BTC dominance with the overall crypto price action.
